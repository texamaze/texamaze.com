Our Products

Our collections come in array of Coir mesh rolls , Jute soil savers, Coir logs, Wattles, Coir garden articles and few verities of Coir mats, Coir ropes etc.

Coir Logs

Coir log is a natural fiber product designed to provide soil stabilization and support along river banks, hillsides, and other erosion prone areas.

Coco Mesh Rolls

They are for all soil bioengineering and erosion control applications in stream banks, slopes, wetlands, hillside soils and golf course development.

Jute Soil Savers

A biodegradable open weave erosion control mesh suitable for short term erosion protection to batters and open drains.

Coir Garden Articles

Improves air porosity in soil, even when wet as well as improving moisture retention.

Coir Door Mats
